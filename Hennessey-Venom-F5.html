Overview

The Hennessey Venom F5 is an American hypercar custom-engineered from the ground up to eliminate compromise, demolish speed barriers, and achieve world-class driving dynamics. Named after the highest category of tornadoes, its structural architecture utilizes an ultra-lightweight carbon fiber chassis designed to minimize mass and withstand extreme mechanical forces.

Engine and Performance

  • Engine: 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, famously code-named "Fury".
  • Power: 1,817 hp and 1,193 lb-ft of torque on standard configurations, scales up to 2,031 hp on specialized Evolution specifications.
  • Top Speed: Engineered to breach the 300 mph (484 km/h) milestone, with ongoing evaluations targeting a 311 mph maximum velocity.
  • Transmission: 7-speed single-clutch automated manual, delivering rapid actuation to the rear wheels.

Aerodynamics and Design

  • Aerodynamic profile shaped to maintain an incredibly low drag coefficient while providing high downforce stability.
  • Full carbon fiber monocoque weighing a mere 86 kg to optimize the power-to-weight matrix.
  • Track-focused iterations feature an elevated rear wing, extended front splitter, and active roof scoops.
  • Minimalist, jet-fighter-inspired cockpit layout prioritizing high-speed ergonomics and bespoke driver controls.
