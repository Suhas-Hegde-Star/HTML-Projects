Overview

The Bugatti Chiron Super Sport is a high-performance hypercar engineered for unprecedented speed and luxury, featuring an aerodynamic design optimized for record-breaking top speeds. It boasts a distinctive aerodynamic profile with a sleek, elongated body that reduces drag and maximizes stability at extreme velocities.

Engine and Performance

  • Engine: 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine.
  • Power: 1,577 hp and 1,180 lb-ft torque, making it one of the most powerful production cars ever.
  • Top Speed: Limited electronically to 273 mph (440 km/h) for safety, with custom versions exceeding 300 mph.
  • Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch DSG for seamless power delivery.

Aerodynamics and Design

  • Extended rear section to improve high-speed stability.
  • Carbon fiber body with active aerodynamic elements.
  • Lightweight design focused on achieving maximum acceleration and speed.
  • Luxurious interior with advanced infotainment and bespoke materials.
