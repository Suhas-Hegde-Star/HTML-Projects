Hennessey Venom F5

Back to Top 10 Fastest Cars

Overview

The Hennessey Venom F5 is an American hypercar engineered to surpass 311 mph (500 km/h). It features a powerful 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine called “Fury” producing 1,817 hp (with an upgrade option boosting it to 2,031 hp), rear-wheel drive, and advanced aerodynamic design focused on ultimate speed performance.

Engine and Performance

  • Engine: 6.6-liter twin-turbo V8 “Fury” engine.
  • Horsepower: 1,817 hp stock, up to 2,031 hp with Evolution package (E85 fuel).
  • Torque: 1,193 lb-ft (stock), 1,445 lb-ft (Evolution).
  • Transmission: 7-speed single-clutch automated manual with paddle shifters.
  • 0-200 mph: 10.3 seconds (Evolution model).
  • Top Speed: Claimed >300 mph (theoretical).
  • Weight: Approx. 2,950 lbs (1,338 kg).

Aerodynamics and Design

  • Active aerodynamics with rear wing (removable on production model).
  • Carbon fiber chassis and body with lightweight components.
  • Optimized shape and drag coefficient of approximately 0.39 on production model.
  • Upgraded suspension with multiple drive modes for ride and handling.

Additional Notes

The Venom F5 also boasts a lightweight structure and advanced engineering innovations that rival the quickest production cars worldwide. The Evolution package adds further power and refinements.
